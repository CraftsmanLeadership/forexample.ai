About Dr. Jesse W. Johnson

DSL, MAOM • Lieutenant Colonel, U.S. Air Force (Retired)

With over two decades of strategic leadership experience in the Department of War, I've witnessed firsthand the challenges contractors face when partnering with military organizations. My mission is to bridge that gap.
